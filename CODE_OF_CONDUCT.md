# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be welcoming for learners, teachers, maintainers, and contributors from any background. We pledge to make participation respectful, constructive, and focused on accessible science learning.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of different experience levels and learning goals.
- Give constructive feedback with concrete suggestions.
- Credit sources, contributors, and prior work.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its educational impact.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Submitting copyrighted assets without permission.
- Presenting stylized educational visuals as clinical or research-grade evidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations can lead to a ban from project spaces.

To report a problem, open a private maintainer contact channel if available, or email the maintainer listed on the GitHub profile. Reports will be handled with care and discretion.

This code is adapted from common open source community standards for small educational projects.
